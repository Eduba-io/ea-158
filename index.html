/ 02 · 60_30_10_frame

Most organizations misread the finding as a closed loop.

Find the vulnerability, file the ticket, ship the patch. At the scale Haize is working, one finding opens three internal conversations at once. The post-report work splits cleanly.

  1. 60
    traditional_code_and_workflow
    Audit logging. Ticket workflow. CI wiring. Release gates.
  2. 30
    rule_based_policy
    Policy mapping. Attestation. MRM sign-off. Regulatory alignment.
  3. 10
    genuine_ai_and_judgment
    Residual-risk acceptance. Ambiguous-finding triage. Policy gaps.
